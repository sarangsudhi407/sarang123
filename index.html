Frequently Asked Questions

Will this knob fit my gas stove?

Yes, Turna is designed with a universal fit mechanism that works with 98% of gas stoves available in India, including major brands like Prestige, Pigeon, and Sunflame.

Does it require batteries or electricity?

No, Turna is completely mechanical. It uses a smart spring-loaded mechanism to ensure safety without needing any external power source.

Is it difficult to install?

Not at all! You can install it yourself in less than 2 minutes. Just pull out your old knob and push Turna in. No tools required.

How does the safety lock work?

Turna requires a "Push & Turn" action. This prevents accidental activation by children or loose clothing, ensuring gas is only released when you intend it.
